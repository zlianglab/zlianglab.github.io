Zhikai Liang Lab @ North Dakota State University, Fargo

We aim to leverage a 3R approach -- Response omics, Response modeling and Response phenotyping -- to uncover the genetics underlying crop resilience to diverse environmental challenges, driving innovations that enhance productivity, resource efficiency, and economic returns for agricultural systems.
